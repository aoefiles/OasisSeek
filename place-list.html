Places

Make Egypt part of your destination itinerary, adventuring through the deserts and one of the richest farm lands in the world.

THE NILE

The Nile, the world's longest river, is a captivating destination that offers a blend of natural beauty, historical richness, and cultural depth. Flowing through 11 countries, its most iconic stretch lies in Egypt, where visitors can explore ancient wonders such as the Pyramids of Giza, the temples of Luxor and Karnak, and the Valley of the Kings. A Nile cruise provides serene views of lush riverbanks, timeless villages, and golden deserts, making it an unforgettable journey through history and nature.

THE RED SEA

The Red Sea is a breathtaking destination renowned for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and rich marine biodiversity. A paradise for divers and snorkelers, it offers unparalleled underwater experiences, with colorful fish, shipwrecks, and pristine coral gardens. Bordering Egypt, Sudan, and Saudi Arabia, the Red Sea is also a gateway to luxurious beach resorts, ancient port cities, and thrilling water sports, making it a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

WADI AL-NATRUN

Wadi Al-Natrun, a serene oasis in Egypt's Western Desert, is a destination steeped in spiritual and natural significance. Known for its ancient Coptic monasteries, this tranquil valley has been a center of Christian monasticism since the 4th century, with beautifully preserved monasteries like Deir Anba Bishoy and Deir El-Suryan. Surrounded by salt lakes and arid landscapes, Wadi Al-Natrun offers a unique blend of historical, religious, and ecological experiences, making it a peaceful retreat for history enthusiasts and spiritual seekers.
